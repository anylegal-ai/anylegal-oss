# Playbook — Standard Positions

This file defines your organization's standard negotiating positions.
The AI agent reads this when reviewing or drafting contracts.

## Indemnification

- **Our position**: Mutual indemnification, capped at contract value
- **Red line**: Uncapped indemnification or indemnification for third-party IP claims without carve-out
- **Acceptable**: Indemnification capped at 1x annual fees with standard carve-outs

## Limitation of Liability

- **Our position**: Mutual cap at 12 months of fees paid/payable
- **Red line**: Unlimited liability or liability exceeding 2x annual fees
- **Acceptable**: Cap at total fees paid in prior 12 months; exclude gross negligence and willful misconduct

## Termination

- **Our position**: Either party may terminate for convenience with 30 days' notice
- **Red line**: No termination for convenience or >90 day notice period
- **Acceptable**: 60-day notice for termination for convenience

## Governing Law

- **Our position**: [Your preferred jurisdiction]
- **Red line**: Jurisdiction with no connection to either party
- **Acceptable**: Jurisdiction of the counterparty if commercially necessary

## Confidentiality

- **Our position**: Mutual confidentiality, 3-year survival post-termination
- **Red line**: Perpetual confidentiality or no carve-outs for legally required disclosure
- **Acceptable**: 2-5 year survival period with standard carve-outs

## IP Ownership

- **Our position**: We retain all pre-existing IP; deliverables assigned upon full payment
- **Red line**: Work-for-hire with no license back
- **Acceptable**: Joint ownership with clear usage rights

## Dispute Resolution

- **Our position**: [Arbitration / Litigation] in [preferred venue]
- **Red line**: Binding arbitration with no right to injunctive relief
- **Acceptable**: Mediation first, then arbitration under [preferred rules]
